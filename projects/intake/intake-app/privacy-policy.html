BiteLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

What We Collect

BiteLens collects the following information to provide our food tracking service:

  • Account info: Email address and password (for authentication)
  • Profile data: Name, age, height, weight, dietary preferences, and fitness goals you provide during onboarding
  • Food entries: Meals you log, including food names, nutritional data, and meal times
  • Photos: Food images you choose to scan are sent to our AI service for analysis. Photos are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers after analysis is complete.
  • Barcode scans: UPC codes you scan to look up food products
  • Purchase info: Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat and Apple/Google. We do not store your payment details.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized calorie and macro tracking
  • To analyze food photos using AI (Anthropic Claude) and return nutritional estimates
  • To calculate recommended daily targets based on your goals
  • To manage your subscription tier (Free, Plus, Pro)

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication (data stored securely in the US)
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Food image and text analysis. Images are processed and not retained by Anthropic after analysis.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and purchase management
  • Open Food Facts: Barcode product lookups (public database, no personal data shared)

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security enabled. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Export: Pro tier users can export their data as PDF

Children's Privacy

BiteLens is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
